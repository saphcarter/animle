Below are the basic instructions on how to play Animle:

  • The aim of the game is to guess the unknown Australian animal from basic hints.
  • You will begin with two hints, and after every incorrect guess, a new hint will be unlocked.
  • Different hints about the animals include:
    • Their classification (marsupial, mammal, reptile, etc.)
    • The number of legs the animal has (if any)
    • If the animal has a tail or not
    • If the animal has wings or not
    • If the animal has fins or flippers or not
    • The rough size category of the animal
    • The climate they live in
    • Their endangered rating
  • Users (like yourself) can login using the 'Login' button at the top of the screen.
  • Once logged in, stats from your recent attempts are available when clicking on the 'Stats' button at the top of the screen.
  • The game allows multiple attempts each day for different animals, and the animals are randomly chosen for each device.
